Karan Patel — UX Strategist

Hi, I’m Karan, I grew up in Nairobi, Kenya, in a completely different hemisphere of the world, but with the all familiar company of Space Jam and Star Wars.

Growing up across multiple cultures, I’ve always been fascinated by the crossovers and clashes that result in the world we live in. This gave me a deep love for learning about what makes people tick and wanting to understand the psychology behind our desires vs. needs.

I’ve used that passion to develop a career doing work that I love with people that I admire and support — to understand complex problems, help find the nuances that matter, and guide changes that I want to see.

As a Strategist at Instrument, I’ve worked on some pretty fulfilling projects, like reworking the entire user experience for Google’s Science Fair, forming a vision for the future of television with Android TV, and defining what it means to talk about racial equality and justice for Salesforce.

I choose to continue pursuing work that is meaningful, advocating for the truth, helping others find their voice, and to become a more compassionate human and strategist.

Open to conversations, collaborations and (virtual) coffee.

✌🏾 Strategist. Advocate. Seeking my truth.
— Karan
