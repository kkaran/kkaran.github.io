Migo: Separation Anxiety

Migo is a 16-Month thesis created with the aim of helping ease the anxiety in children going to school for their first time. The intentions were to step away from the use of prescription medicine, and help ease anxiety with a relatable solution.

Winner: ACIDO Rocket Award, Best in Category (Educational Tools)
