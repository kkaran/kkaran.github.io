Who am I?




My name is Karan and I'm a UI & UX Designer living in Chicago. Industrial Design is my background. However,
I currently help design and develop platforms within ecosystems that address visuals and interactions to help in ideal user experiences.

Born in Nairobi, Kenya and raised in Toronto, I've had a diverse upbringing and it has employed me a unique perspective on life. I constantly push the boundaries of my knowledge and skills with the goal of eventually being able to apply myself towards improving the quality of human lives.

I'm a graduate of OCAD University, where I found myself often exploring different processes and gaining knowledge in everything design. I am intensely relentless in my work to ensure my results are desirable.
