Who am I?




My name is Karan and I'm a UI & UX Designer with a background in Industrial Design , currently in Chicago. I help design and develop platforms, within ecosystems that address visuals and interactions to help in ideal user experiences.

Born in Nairobi, Kenya and raised in Toronto, I've had a diverse upbringing and it has employed me a unique perspective on life. I constantly push the boundaries of my knowledge and skills with the goal of eventually being able to apply myself towards improving the quality of human lives.
