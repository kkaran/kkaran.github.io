The Hobbit: Part 1

The Hobbit is an upcoming film series consisting of three epic fantasy-adventure films directed, co-written and produced by Peter Jackson and based on J. R. R. Tolkien's fantasy novel The Hobbit.

Release date: December 14, 2012 (USA)

  • Director:Peter Jackson
  • Music:Howard Shore
  • Screenplay:Fran Walsh, Philippa Boyens, Guillermo del Toro, Peter Jackson
  • Producers:Carolynne Cunningham, Peter Jackson, Fran Walsh
