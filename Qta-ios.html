Concepts

From the early stages, my role was to redesign the core iOS experience of the Questia app; using user based research as a launching point to start conceptualizing.

Typography & Color

Using Questia's web style guide as a reference, we created a set of patterns to apply for an iOS friendly UI.

UI Mockups

Testing and refining the UI was an important part of the process. Using hi-fidelity mockups, we tested interactions to differentiate the useful from the intrusive, and to validate what was most important to our users

Questia Research v. 4.0

After months of designing, developing and testing, we succesfully reached a point where we could submit our app. At this stage, I was responsible for creating all marketing assets in order to launch our app in the store.
